Our Organization

About ScamEducation.org

We are an independent group dedicated to equipping communities with the knowledge to stay safe from fraud.

Our Story

Founded on a Simple Belief

ScamEducation.org was founded by a cybersecurity specialist who saw firsthand through community outreach how scams can devastate individuals, families, and communities both financially and emotionally.

We believe accurate, accessible information is the most powerful tool for preventing scams. Cybersecurity specialists create our materials with accessibility in mind so everyone can use them.

Mission Statement

To prevent financial fraud through free, trusted, plain-language education that empowers people and communities to recognize and resist scams.

Stronger Against Scams.

How We Work

Our Core Principles

Every decision we make is guided by these commitments to the people we serve.

1

Accuracy

All content is created and reviewed by cybersecurity specialists. We only publish information we can verify through official sources.

2

Dignity

People affected by fraud deserve compassion, not judgment. We never use shame or fear. We provide clear, respectful information that helps people stay safe.

3

Accessibility

Our materials use large print, plain language, and high-contrast design. We create everything so people of all technical skill levels can use it.
